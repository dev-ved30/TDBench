Getting to Chicago

By Air

O'Hare International Airport (ORD) is the primary airport serving Chicago, located approximately 14 miles from downtown.

Midway International Airport (MDW) is a secondary option, located about 10 miles from downtown.

Both airports offer convenient CTA train connections to downtown Chicago.

By Train

Chicago Union Station is served by Amtrak with connections from across the country.

The station is located in downtown Chicago, accessible by taxi, rideshare, or public transit to the conference hotel.

Local Transportation

Chicago offers excellent public transportation via the CTA (trains and buses).

Rideshare services (Uber, Lyft) and taxis are readily available throughout the city.

The Magnificent Mile area is very walkable.

Visa Information

International participants may require a visa to enter the United States. We recommend checking visa requirements well in advance of the workshop.

If you need an invitation letter for visa purposes, please contact the organizing committee at contact@tdabench.org.
