Additional Information

Program Notes

Contributed Talks

Contributed talks are 15 minutes plus 5 minutes for questions. Please submit your abstract by January 15, 2026 for consideration.

Poster Sessions

Posters should be A0 size (portrait orientation). Poster boards and materials will be provided. Setup begins at 15:30 on Day 2.

Working Groups

Participants will be assigned to working groups based on their expertise and interests indicated during registration.
